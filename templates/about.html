About StyleLane

Overview

StyleLane is a scalable cloud-based inventory management and reporting system designed specifically for fashion retailers. It provides a comprehensive solution for managing multi-store inventory, tracking stock trends, and automating communication with suppliers to prevent delays and stockouts.

Key Features

  • Role-Based Dashboards: Customized interfaces for Admins, Store Managers, and Suppliers
  • Real-Time Stock Updates: Track inventory levels across all store locations instantly
  • Automated Restock Requests: Streamline communication between stores and suppliers
  • Sales Reporting: Data-driven insights into retail performance
  • Low-Stock Alerts: Automated notifications to prevent stockouts

Our Mission

StyleLane increases operational efficiency and supply chain transparency by automating manual workflows and providing data-driven insights into retail performance. We help fashion retailers maintain optimal inventory levels, reduce waste, and ensure products are always available when customers need them.

Technology

Built with Flask and SQLite, StyleLane offers a robust and reliable platform that can easily scale to cloud infrastructure. The system is designed with security and performance in mind, ensuring your inventory data is safe and accessible when you need it.
